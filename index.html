Why does my drain smell like sewage?

Usually caused by a dried-out P-trap, a cracked sewer vent pipe, or a partial sewer line blockage. Try running water for 30 seconds to refill the P-trap. If the smell persists, call a professional for a sewer line inspection.

What causes pipes to burst in Birmingham?

Occasional freezes can burst pipes in uninsulated areas like crawl spaces and exterior walls. Insulate exposed pipes, keep cabinet doors open during freezes, and let faucets drip slightly during extreme cold.
